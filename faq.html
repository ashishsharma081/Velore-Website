Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our most commonly asked questions below.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

How do I start a return or exchange?

Log into your account, go to Order History, select the item, and choose "Return" or "Exchange."

When will I get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item.

Can I exchange an item for a different size?

Yes! You can exchange items for a different size through your account.

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and debit cards.

Can I apply a promo code or gift card at checkout?

Yes! Enter your promo code or gift card number during checkout and click "Apply."

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placing them.

Do you offer pre-orders?

Yes! Selected items are available for pre-order with estimated shipping dates.

Shipping & Delivery

Where do you ship?

We ship to all 50 US states and international destinations including Canada, UK, Europe, and Australia.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express and overnight options are available.

How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

What if my package is lost or delayed?

Contact our customer support team and we'll locate your package or arrange a replacement.

Sizing & Fit

How do I know what size to order?

Check our Size Guide on each product page with measurements for chest, waist, hips, and inseam.

Do your clothes run true to size?

Yes, our clothes generally run true to size. Check "Fit Notes" on each product page for specifics.

What if I'm between sizes?

We recommend sizing up for comfort. Check customer reviews for fit feedback.

Can I return or exchange if it doesn't fit?

Absolutely! We offer free exchanges and accept returns within 30 days.
